    1/18/2012 1 Check-in Here

    My first time here, and I really like this place. The staff is friendly and personable and make you feel comfortable. Before my massage began, we discussed where I was experiencing pain. Right at the beginning, she checked whether I like soft, med or firm pressure. After getting a sense of what was going on with my back muscles, the therapist confirmed with me and laid out plan for the rest of the session.
    I really loved how the table was low and the therapist could work from above. I think this allowed for her to apply the optimal pressure. When she pressed on my muscles, my skin felt tingly and it was like a nerve stimulation overload (in a good way). Also, I've never had my ears massaged before, but I liked it. When you come here, be specific about what you like. Ex: I like my hands massaged, but not really my feet. They seem totally willing to tailor every session to what the customer requests. Prices are super cheap for such a quality massage. I've overpaid for a crappy massage before, and it's not fun.
    Scheduling was extremely easy-all online with email confirmations.
    I plan on returning!

About This Business

Provided by business

Specialties

If relaxation or pain relief is what you are seeking, then let us take care of you! The Peaceful Warrior Massage, LLC is NOT an assembly line massage clinic. We provide focused massage therapy to every client by being present in the "here and now." We use therapeutic modalities to include deep tissue, myofascial release, sports massage and trigger point therapy to relieve pain, reduce stress, and improve the quality of life of our clients. We strive to educate everyone we talk to about the many benefits of therapeutic massage in regards to overall health and prevention.
As a Veteran Owned business, and our way to say "Thank You", we offer special rates for military, law enforcement, fire fighters and EMS. ALWAYS $40 - 1 hour and $60 - 90 minute sessions. Teachers are always $45 - 1 hour and  $65 - 90 minutes. Also ask us about on-site chair massage! Perfect for the office, retirement parties, bridal showers, birthdays and fundraisers!

Meet the Business Owner: Scott R.

I first became interested with massage therapy in 1992. I was a soldier in the Army and studying Russian at the Defense Language Institute in Monterey, CA. It can be a very stressful school and, on occasion, a fellow student would plant themselves in front of me while we watched TV in the barracks. I really enjoyed the positive feedback I received and was encouraged to go to school to do bodywork professionally. I attended the Monterey Institute of Touch. Six months later, I moved out of state. After 17 years of pursuing other careers and employment, I decided it was important to return to school and do what I truly enjoy and am passionate about - massage therapy.

I am certified in neuromuscular therapy and myofascial techniques. I have been trained in chair massage, hot stone massage, and sports massage. My goals include providing a great place for clients to come for bodywork as well as an environment that will attract the right therapists for our clients.
